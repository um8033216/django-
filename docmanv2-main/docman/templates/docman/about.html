Software

The Presentation Management site and on-site computers have Office 2010 installed and will support all versions of PowerPoint.

Fonts

All fonts that are included with Office 2010 are supported. If you need a specialized font, it should be embedded into your PowerPoint presentation. Click here for explanation of this process.

Videos

If you are uploading a video, you should upload it at the same time as your presentation.

Please remember to compress your videos. Your video will upload faster and load faster during your presentation with no distinguishable loss in video quality. If you plan to play a DVD as part of your presentation, please notify a technician in the Speaker Ready Room so arrangements can be made in your presentation room.

Speaker Ready Rooom
